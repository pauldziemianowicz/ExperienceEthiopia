3 Day Hiking Tour

Trip Itinerary

Day 1

Travel Route:

  

Gondar > Sankaber Camp

Total Travel Distance:

  

~ 145km/90miles

Hiking Distance:

  

10 miles?

Hiking Duration

  

3-4 Hours

Altitude Change:

  

2133m to 3250m (+ 1117m/3665ft)

Starting from Gondar, we will drive for 2 hours through the scenic foothills of the Simien Mountains. Upon arrival at the park headquarters in Debark we will arrange our park permits and pick up our scout. We will then drive for another hour to Sankaber camp. From Sankaber, we will start our first day with a 3-4 hour acclimatization hike along the northern edge of the escarpment. You will have your first opportunity to see some of the world-famous views over the foothills and plains of northern Ethiopia, and you will Encounter Gelada Baboons along the hike and at Sankaber campsite. We will spend the night at Sankaber campsite.

Day 2

Travel Route:

  

Sankaber Camp > Gich Camp

Total Travel Distance:

  

?

Hiking Distance:

  

?

Hiking Duration

  

5-6 Hours

Altitude Change:

  

3250 to 3600 (+ 350m/1148ft)

We will depart Sankaber Camp after breakfast, walking through extensive areas of grassland and Heather Forest. Along the route we will enjoy stunning views along the escarpment. We will stop at Nigus Aysimush viewpoint, which offers a spectacular view. We will also stop to admire Jinbar Waterfall, where the Jinbar river drops nearly 500 meters off the plateau and plunges to the valley below. If we are lucky, we will see Menelik’s Bushbuck, Klipspringer, and possibly even a Leopard (if we are extremely lucky). The entertaining gelada baboons will be seen along the route. We will then stop for lunch on the banks of the Jinbar River. We will then ascend to the settlement of Gich. As we pass through the village, we can have coffee with a local family in their traditional Tukul hut that is shared with their animals – this is a great way to get a glimpse of the day-to-day life of the locals! We will settle in, eat dinner, and hopefully we will get to see the herd of several hundred Gelada Baboons retreat over the edge of the cliffs where they sleep to avoid predators on the plateau.

Day 3

Travel Route:

  

Gich Camp > Ambaras > Gondar

Total Travel Distance:

  

?

Hiking Distance:

  

?

Hiking Duration

  

5-6 Hours

Altitude Change:

  

3600m to 2133m (- 1467m/4813ft)

Hike from Gich to Ambaras, about 5 to 6 hours. On the way we visit Imet Gogo, the single most spectacular spot in the whole of the Simien mountains. Imet Gogo, at 3926m, has 360 degree views of canyons, cliffs and valleys that look as if they go on forever. From Ambaras we drive back to Gondar.
